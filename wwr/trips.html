At the Black River, you can come have a good time. Come chill with an amazing view, relax and enjoy a quiet ride. Here, you will be guided through quiet waters so that you can enjoy your time and be relaxed.

If you like wonderful landscapes with a little bit more of adventure, the Fun Forest is for you. You can not only enjoy a wonderful view, but have a little challenge and fun navigating through this marvelous river with clear waters

If you are looking for an adventure and a challenge, Speedy River is definitely your place. Here you will not have a chill time, here you will have to test your habilities and skills navigating through this rapids. However, you can still be sure that you will be safe. We have a team of experts following with rescue kits, in case you fall.
